Ingredients

    4 servings
  • 3 1/2 cups flour
  • 5 g yeast (half a packet)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 1/2 tbs sugar
  • 1-2 litre oil

  • Warm water

Method


    Step 1
Cover the dough balls and allow them to rise again in a warm spot (10+ minutes). This second rise gives a nice texture
    Step 2
Mix lukewarm water, sugar, and yeast in a small bowl. Let stand until yeast softens and begins to bubble slightly, about 5 minutes
    Step 3
Sift flour and salt together in a large bowl.
    Step 4
Pour water mixture over flour mixture and knead until dough is smooth and elastic, 5 to 7 minutes. Cover bowl with clean cloth and let dough rise until until doubled in volume, about 45 minutes.
    Step 5
Roll dough until smooth and at least 1 inch thick. Use a size 3 inch biscuit cutter to cut in equal sizes. Repeat with remaining dough.
    Step 6
Drain well and then place on paper towels to absorb the oil.
    Step 7
Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 175Â°C. Fry the dough balls in the hot oil, 2 to 3 pieces at a time, until golden brown, about 3 minutes per side. You can see when the bottom half is brown and that's when you turn them over.
    Step 8
Serve stuffed with curried or savoury mince, butter and jam or syrup. Whatever you want really, and any type of curry is fine.
